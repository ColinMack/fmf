Fund My Future

Because Piggy Banks Only Take You So Far.

What can I invest in?

1) 529 Plan (Tax-advantaged savings plan for college) 2) BlackRock Funds 3) Robo Advised (Powered by FutureAdvisor)

Who can sign up?

Anyone can signup for a Fund My Future Test account. You have to be 18 to open a live funding campaign.

What fee's are there?

Our management fees vary depending on what account is setup. One can be opened for as little as 0.05%.

When can I acess my funds?

The lockout period is chosen upon sign up and will vary per account.

Can I roll my account over?

Yes! After the funds lockout period, the funds can be rolled into any similar investment vehicle. Similar account can also be rolled into your Fund My Future account.

What kind of reporting will I get?

Fund My Future has comprehensive dashboards showing all your current performance metrics. We'll also send a quarterly statement to your email for more detailed analysis.

How do I add money to my account?

A simple linking of your bank account will allow additional funding beyond what your campaign generates.
